In Loving Memory of Willem Oberholzer

Willem Isinyathelo House stands as a living tribute to Willem Oberholzer, Rina's beloved father who passed away 14 years ago. This student boarding home was founded in his memory, embodying the values of care, guidance, and nurturing support that he exemplified throughout his life.

A Father's Legacy

Willem Oberholzer believed deeply in the power of education and the importance of providing young people with a safe, nurturing environment to grow. As a devoted father who understood the needs of children, he recognized that every child deserves both a solid foundation and compassionate guidance along their path.

The name "Isinyathelo" – meaning "step" or "footstep" in Zulu – perfectly captures his philosophy: that true education involves walking alongside students, guiding them step by step, just as he guided his own family with love and wisdom.

Our boarding home continues his legacy by providing primary and high school students with the safe, affordable, and supportive Christian environment that Willem believed every child deserves. We honor his memory by creating a home where young students can thrive academically, spiritually, and personally.

Every aspect of Willem Isinyathelo House reflects the values Willem Oberholzer lived by: integrity, compassion, faith, and a commitment to helping others reach their full potential. We walk with each student on their educational journey, providing the guidance and support that transforms a house into a home.
